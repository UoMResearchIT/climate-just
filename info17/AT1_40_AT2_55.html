Description

Reference

Theme

Vulnerability

Hazard reference

Flood and Heat

Dimension

Ability to Respond (flood) and Ability to Recover (heat)

Domain

Mobility

Indicator

Working away from home (% not working at home)

Assumption

Areas with higher proportions of people working away from home are more likely to be communities with relatively slow responses to heat-wave and flood events (or associated warnings) compared with communities where there are higher proportions of home workers.

Confidence level

Medium

Guidance for the use of this indicator

Adaptation needs to address the specific challenges associated with places where high proportions of people may be away from home during flood events.
