Fuel Poverty (LIHC definition)

The map shows the percentage of households in each local authority (or lsoa) which are defined as being in fuel poverty, under the Low Income High Costs definition. The data is for 2012 and 2014, which is the most recent data available at the time of production of the map. Source data for 2012 can be found at https://www.gov.uk/government/statistics/2012-sub-regional-fuel-poverty-data-low-income-high-costs-indicator

Source data for 2014 can be found at https://www.gov.uk/government/statistics/2014-sub-regional-fuel-poverty-data-low-income-high-costs-indicator

High

Guidance for the use of this indicator

The indicator can be used to identify areas with high levels of fuel poverty.
